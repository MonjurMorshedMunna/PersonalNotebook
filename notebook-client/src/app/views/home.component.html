Welcome to Personal Notebook


This app helps you to revise your day to day learned topics, so that you need little effort to remember them in future. The app keep tracks of your learning resources.

Methodologies

This app follows a specific mechanism to help you doing revision in the most effective methods.
Researchers have found that, the best way to rember a thing is, by doing revision in the following basis:

  • Day after the study
  • One week after the study
  • One month after the study
  • Yearly revision
